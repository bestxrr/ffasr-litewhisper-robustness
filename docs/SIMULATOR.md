# Simulator

Initial priorities:

1. RIR convolution.
2. Additive noise and competing speech.
3. Spectral filtering.
4. Clipping and loudness transforms.

Initial condition distribution:

- dry or RIR-only: 20%
- high severity: 15%
- mid severity: 30%
- low severity: 35%

Initial SNR ranges:

- high: 14 to 25 dB
- mid: 8 to 12 dB
- low: -3 to 6 dB

All sampled augmentation parameters must be logged per utterance.
